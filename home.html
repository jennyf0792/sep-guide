Hello again friend,
I hope the trip to the info section wasn't too hard on you. Where did we leave off again? Oh yes, What is Software Engineering. A software engineer is a licensed professional engineer who is schooled and skilled in the application of engineering discipline to the creation of software. A software engineer is often confused with a programmer, but the two are vastly different disciplines.

Helpful Tips

To be one of the best students is not creating an awe-aspiring website or cool design, it's about the skills and techniques to accomplish your mission. Gather all your confidence together and begin from scratch of what you want to create.

    sketch out and plan what the contents
    ask for advice among your peers or teacher(s)
    remember, have inspiration but don't copy and paste; learn from the code and apply parts of it on your own

Hopefully, you're able to learn great new things along the way of your tough journey. Feel free to send us emails about more advices that you'd like to officially put up on the website! Thank you! Continue being persisent and awesome.

Handy Resources

Some handy resources are practically everywhere. For any questions that your peers cannot answer or your teacher is occupied with another student, then Google is your best friend! You can Google anything and find anything to fits the answer to your question(s).

Strategies

Planning (pseudocode), What is pseudocode? It's writing or creating a picture of your end result before you finish How is it helpful? It gives you an idea of where to start, so you're not lost and can start coding immediately

Tinkering, What is tinkering Tinkering is getting to know the code from different resources How is it helpful? You start by taking pieces and making small changes to see their effects and Learn to apply it to your own project
